# Mission

Podcast Search helps podcast listeners discover, evaluate, and revisit podcasts they love.

## Problem

Finding quality podcasts is hard. Discovery relies on opaque platform algorithms, word of mouth, or top-ten lists that don't reflect personal taste. Listeners have no trusted place to read or write honest reviews, and no tool that learns what they actually enjoy.

## Solution

A fast, beautiful search engine for podcasts where listeners can:

- **Search** the full podcast catalog by title, topic, host, or keyword
- **Review** episodes and shows to guide other listeners
- **Discover** new podcasts through a recommendation engine tuned to their taste

## Who it's for

Podcast listeners who want more control over what they hear next — from casual listeners exploring a new genre to enthusiasts who follow dozens of shows.

## Principles

1. **Listener-first** — every feature serves the person looking for their next great listen, not advertisers or creators.
2. **Honest signal** — reviews and ratings reflect real listener opinion, not paid placement.
3. **Fast and reliable** — search results load instantly; the site never goes down during peak listening hours.
4. **Modern and accessible** — the UI looks great in any modern browser and on mobile.
